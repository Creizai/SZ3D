about studio z 3d

Studio Z is a small but talented studio specializing in Unreal Engine development and Art for Games.

ONLINE MULTIPLAYER

FULLY INTEGRATED

Experts in creating competitive online eSports in Unreal Engine. This team was born out of the necessity for an FPS Online Shooter template that wasn't just "good enough" for the marketplace... but that met our standards and desires. We achieved this goal with our UE4 FPS Template Project, AWS Gamelift Plugin, and The Pro Leagues Back End System.

TECHNICAL ART

BRINGING IMAGINATION TO LIFE

Studio Z 3D is proficient and interested in all of the latest generation, cutting edge, art tools. Utilizing Artificial Intelligence as well as old-school methods we use the best tool for the job to take your creative vision and make it real. We've worked with artists in all industries and we're always interested in new and unique ideas!

DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

CONSULTING AND CREATING

Studio Z 3D consists of a talented team of artists and developers. We can help you with consulting, designing, and even development into the final product. With decades of experience we're well suited from the early consulting phase all the way to shipping the final product.
